3. Exclusions

While we strive to make your safari experience seamless and memorable, there are certain exclusions to be aware of. Please take note of the following:

1. Airport Taxes, International Airfares, and Visas:

Please be advised that airport taxes, international airfares, and visas are not included in the package. It is the responsibility of each traveler to ensure compliance with entry requirements and to cover any associated costs.

2. Insurances:

Trip cancellation and medical insurances are recommended but are not part of the package. We encourage all participants to obtain suitable travel insurance to safeguard against unforeseen circumstances.

3. Premium Drinks/Beverages with Meals:

Enjoying premium drinks and beverages with meals is not included in the package. Any additional beverage preferences beyond the standard offerings will be at the expense of the traveler.

4. Tips and Gratuities:

Tipping is a personal gesture and is not included in the package. Kindly consider gratuities for the driver and hotel staff as a separate arrangement during your safari.

5. Hot Air Balloon Rides or Excursions Not Mentioned on Itinerary:

For those seeking additional adventures such as hot air balloon rides or excursions not mentioned on the itinerary, please note that these activities are not covered in the package and can be arranged separately based on individual preferences.

We want your safari to be worry-free, and being informed about these exclusions ensures a transparent and enjoyable experience. If you have any questions or require further clarification, feel free to reach out to our team.
